2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

3. How We Do NOT Use Your Information

We want to be clear about what we do not do with your data:

7. Children's Privacy

Vysti is used in educational settings that may include children under 13. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA).

Age verification

We collect date of birth during registration to verify age. Users under 13 are not permitted to create accounts on their own. If a user indicates they are under 13 during registration, account creation is blocked and they are directed to have a parent, guardian, or teacher create an account on their behalf.

How children's accounts are created

  • Parent or guardian enrollment. A parent or legal guardian may create an account for their child. By doing so, the parent provides verifiable consent for us to collect and use their child's information as described in this Privacy Policy, solely for educational purposes.
  • Teacher or school enrollment. Under COPPA's school official exception, a teacher or school administrator may enroll students in a class. By doing so, the school acts as the parent's agent and represents that it has obtained any required parental notice or consent under applicable law, and that the Service is used solely for educational purposes.

Information collected from children

We collect the same categories of information from child accounts as described in Section 1 above. We use this information only to provide the educational Service. We do not use children's information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the educational Service.

Parental rights

Parents and guardians may at any time:

  • Review the personal information we have collected from their child.
  • Request that we delete their child's personal information and account.
  • Refuse to permit further collection or use of their child's information.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@vysti.org.

Unauthorized child accounts

If we learn that a child under 13 has created an account without verifiable parental or school consent, we will suspend the account and delete the associated personal information promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has created an account without proper consent, please contact us immediately.
