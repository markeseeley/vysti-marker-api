In a scene, a girl moved to a new city due to an accident in her family and it suddenly changed her life because she met a friend which helped her to live a much better life in the future. A sudden chance can alter people's life because people may encounter different things that they will not even know if they didn't meet the chance. However, some argues that people's destiny can only be shaped by people's long-term efforts. From my perspective, it is true that single events can alter people's life because it can provide people opportunities that can benefit them for life. However, a single event is not able to shape people's life if they don't consider the chance as significant and put more effort to continue to make use of the opportunities.

Primarily, a single event can change people's life because it provides them opportunities. One example lies in the Narrative of the Life of Fredrick Douglass. In the book, Douglass went to Baltimore accidentally. During that time, he learnt to read and write with the other children. Because of this knowledge, he can escape from slavery and reveal the cruelty of slavery in his book. If Douglass did not go to Baltimore, he would not gain any knowledge about abolishing slavery and the technique of how to read and write. As a result, he might be a slave for his

whole life but not successfully escaped from slavery and became a famous people who fight against slavery and uncovered the darkness of slavery in front of the world. This shows how a single event can change people's life because it enables to people to get access to different knowledge and experience. Additionally, my father's personal experience can also support the claim. When my father graduated from college, he suddenly got an opportunity to travel to Shenzhen and did not need to pay any money for a airplane. In his trip, he met one of his best friend for the whole life. They have the similar ideas about the world and their future, so my father decided to stay in shenzhen and established his own business with his friend. Now, with this surprise chance, he earns a lot of money and receives better working opportunities and resources. These resources, without the help of his friend, can not gained by my father himself. If would hadn't come to Shenzhen, maybe he will get a normal job and spend his whole life on it. From these examples, we can obviously realized how opportuni a single event can change people's life.

This perspective, while it is compelling, when people have use of this chance or event turns to be invalid when people can not recognized fully

its importance and make use of it. Most evidently, in the book A Raisin In The Sun, Wad Mama gave a a lot of money, which is the insurance money of Big Walter, to Walter to achieve his dream However, because Walter did not store this money in the bank and made use of it carefully, this money is being stolen by his "friend". Consequently, Walter can not use this money to establish the liquor store he wants but to continue to do the job he do don't like. From this evidence, we can see how people's careless and lack of conscience can led the opportunity to be not valuable, which link back to the topic sentence. Another example is also how people's lack of consideration can led can make the chance useless. My uncle, who was really talented when he was young, has been selected to go to a competition. If he can win, he can not only gain a huge amount of money but also go abroad to study. Because of others appreciating to my uncle, he didn't spend a long lot of time to prepare for the competition. At last, he missed the price. He was shocked about the result because he thought he will win easily. By using this example, it present how people's lack of efforts can make the opportunity be

useless. Although he seized the opportunity, he still can not alter his life and live a more comfortable life. As a famous sentence said: "Opportunities are for those who prepared". Hence, sometimes a single event can not change people's life if they don't consider it as valuable and put more efforts on it.
